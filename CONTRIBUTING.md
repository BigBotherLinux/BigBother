# Contributing to BigBother

Contributions are welcome — provided they meet a certain level of inconvenience.

## Reporting Issues and Ideas

Use the issue templates:

- **[Bug report](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/bug_report.md)** — something works correctly and you want it fixed
- **[Feature request](.github/ISSUE_TEMPLATE/feature_request.md)** — a new way to make things worse

## Contribution Guidelines

- Features should **not** spark joy
- It has to be the right kind of wrong, if that makes sense
- The system must remain **usable**
- Should **not cause data loss** or be destructive
- Think of the entire user experience, it should be death by a thousand cuts, not one big bang
- Must be intentional and not just feel broken
- Must not depend on external services that could go down
- The ISO must be reproducible and the installer should not contain unfree software, however unfree software is allowed post-installation

## Maintainer Veto

@hauskens has final say on contributions. Pull requests may be rejected, but keep in mind its the user we intend to inconvenience, not the contributors.
